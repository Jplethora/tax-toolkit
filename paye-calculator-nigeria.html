What the estimate includes

  • A simplified consolidated relief estimate commonly used in educational tax examples.
  • Nigerian personal income tax bands from 7 percent to 24 percent.
  • A minimum-tax floor for awareness where the computed liability is very low.

What it does not include

  • Special exemptions, treaty issues, or state-specific administrative differences.
  • Complex payroll adjustments, arrears, or tax credits.
  • Professional advice for filings, disputes, or audits.
