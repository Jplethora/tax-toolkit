PAYE Calculator Nigeria

Short answer: Enter your monthly salary to estimate how much PAYE tax you pay and your take-home salary.

This calculator uses standard Nigerian tax rules including CRA and pension deductions to give you a clear estimate.

How to use this calculator

  • Enter your monthly salary
  • Click calculate
  • View your tax breakdown and net salary

What this calculation includes

  • Consolidated Relief Allowance (CRA)
  • Pension contribution (8%)
  • Nigeria progressive tax bands

This is an estimate based on standard assumptions. Actual tax may vary depending on salary structure and additional deductions.

Not sure if your tax is correct?

If your actual deductions are different from this result, it may be due to allowances or adjustments.
