Personal Income Tax in Nigeria Explained

Personal Income Tax (PIT) is the tax paid by individuals on income earned within Nigeria. It is administered by state internal revenue services.

Who Pays Personal Income Tax

The following individuals may be subject to personal income tax:

  • Employees and salary earners
  • Self-employed individuals
  • Freelancers and consultants
  • Business owners and traders

Sources of Taxable Income

Common sources of taxable income include:

  • Salaries and wages
  • Business profits
  • Professional fees
  • Rental income
  • Investment income

Understanding Tax Compliance

Individuals may be required to file annual tax returns and maintain proper records of income and allowable deductions.

Why Tax Records Matter

Maintaining proper financial records helps individuals understand their tax exposure and remain compliant with applicable tax laws.
