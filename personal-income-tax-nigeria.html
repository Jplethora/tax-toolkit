Personal income tax

How personal income tax works in Nigeria, in practical terms.

This page gives a calmer overview of what personal income tax means, who it can affect, and why readers should care even before filing season arrives.

Jerisma Plethora Team

Built for Nigerian residents, freelancers, business owners, and diaspora families who want the topic explained plainly before they pay for help.

Personal Income Tax applies to income earned by individuals, but the way it shows up depends on how the money is earned. Employees usually meet it through payroll deductions, while self-employed individuals often experience it through filing and record keeping.

Income types that can matter

  • Salary and wages.
  • Business and professional income.
  • Freelance and consulting income.
  • Rental or investment-related income in some cases.

Employees versus self-employed readers

Employees often feel the tax through PAYE. Self-employed individuals, freelancers, and founders usually have a more active role in calculating, documenting, and filing.

Why the TIN still matters

A Tax Identification Number is often one of the foundation pieces of a cleaner tax relationship. That is why the TIN guide sits near the top of this site.
