Tax for Small Businesses in Nigeria

Small businesses form a large part of Nigeria’s economy. Understanding basic tax obligations can help business owners maintain compliance and avoid penalties.

1. Types of Small Businesses

Examples of small businesses include:

  • Retail traders
  • Online businesses
  • Service providers
  • Consultants and professionals

2. Common Tax Types

Small businesses may encounter several types of taxes depending on their activities.

  • Personal Income Tax for sole proprietors
  • Company Income Tax for registered companies
  • Value Added Tax (VAT)
  • Withholding tax considerations

3. Importance of Record Keeping

Maintaining proper records helps business owners understand income, expenses, and potential tax obligations.

Important records may include:

  • Sales records
  • Expense receipts
  • Bank transaction history
  • Inventory documentation
