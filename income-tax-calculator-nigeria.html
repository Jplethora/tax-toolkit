Nigeria Income Tax Calculator

Estimate your total tax if you earn from salary, freelance, or business income.

How to use

  • Enter your annual income (not monthly)
  • Include all income sources
  • Enter pension if applicable
  • Click calculate

Example

If you earn ₦2,000,000 annually and contribute ₦200,000 pension, your tax is calculated after applying CRA and tax bands.

👉 Use PAYE calculator for salary-only income
