How to File Tax Returns in Nigeria

Filing tax returns is an important part of tax compliance for individuals and businesses in Nigeria.

Tax returns help tax authorities understand income earned during the year and determine any tax obligations.

1. Who May Need to File Tax Returns

  • Salary earners
  • Self-employed individuals
  • Freelancers and consultants
  • Business owners and traders

2. Information Typically Required

Tax returns usually require information such as:

  • Sources of income
  • Business profits
  • Employment earnings
  • Allowable deductions

3. Record Keeping

Maintaining organized records may help individuals understand their tax obligations and complete their filings more easily.

Examples include:

  • Income records
  • Expense documentation
  • Bank statements
  • Employment income summaries
