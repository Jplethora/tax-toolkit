1. Tax Obligations for Freelancers

Freelancers in Nigeria may be treated as self-employed individuals. Income earned from freelance work may fall under personal income tax rules.

2. Income Sources

Common income sources for freelancers include:

  • Software development
  • Graphic design
  • Digital marketing
  • Consulting services
  • Content creation

3. Record Keeping

Freelancers are generally encouraged to maintain clear records of:

  • Income received
  • Invoices issued
  • Business expenses
  • Bank transactions

Understanding basic tax obligations can help freelancers avoid compliance issues and maintain proper financial records.
