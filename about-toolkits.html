Nigeria Tax Information Toolkits

Educational resources for residents, diaspora Nigerians, and investors

About the Toolkits

The Nigeria Tax Information Toolkits are practical educational resources designed to help individuals, professionals, and business owners better understand common tax obligations connected to Nigeria.

They were created to simplify complex tax topics and provide structured guidance on areas where many Nigerians often have questions — especially in light of recent tax reforms and increasing compliance requirements.

These resources focus on clear explanations, practical examples, and structured guidance rather than technical legal language.

Who These Toolkits Are For

Nigerian Residents

The Resident Toolkit is designed for:

  • Salary earners
  • Freelancers and remote workers
  • Small business owners
  • Professionals with multiple income streams
  • Individuals trying to understand tax obligations

Topics include:

  • Personal income tax awareness
  • Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • Basic tax filing guidance
  • Freelancer and contractor tax considerations
  • Common compliance mistakes

Nigerians in the Diaspora & Foreign Investors

The Diaspora Toolkit is designed for:

  • Nigerians living abroad
  • Diaspora individuals sending funds to Nigeria
  • Foreign investors with Nigerian assets
  • Individuals with cross-border income or property

Topics include:

  • Tax exposure connected to Nigerian income
  • Property and investment considerations
  • Remittances and financial transfers
  • Residency considerations
  • Common diaspora tax questions

What You Will Find Inside

  • Simple educational guides
  • Practical worksheets
  • Compliance awareness checklists
  • Frequently asked questions
  • Reference notes on Nigerian tax obligations
