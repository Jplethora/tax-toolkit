PAYE guide

How PAYE tax is commonly calculated in Nigeria.

This page explains the broad structure behind salary deductions so employees and employers can understand what the tax calculation is trying to do before they focus on the exact numbers.

Jerisma Plethora Team

Built for Nigerian residents, freelancers, business owners, and diaspora families who want the topic explained plainly before they pay for help.

PAYE stands for Pay-As-You-Earn. In practice, it is the payroll method used to deduct personal income tax from an employee's earnings and remit the tax to the relevant authority.

A simple flow for understanding PAYE

  1. Start with annual gross income.
  2. Consider pension and other relevant deductions.
  3. Apply the consolidated relief allowance using a simplified model.
  4. Calculate taxable income.
  5. Apply the tax bands to the taxable amount.
