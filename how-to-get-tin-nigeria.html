How To Get TIN in Nigeria (Step-by-Step Guide)

A Tax Identification Number (TIN) is the official number used by Nigerian tax authorities to identify individuals and businesses for tax purposes.

Every taxpayer in Nigeria is expected to have a TIN, whether you are:

  • An employee
  • A freelancer
  • A business owner
  • A company registered with CAC
  • A Nigerian living abroad with Nigerian income

Official Government Portal

The Joint Tax Board provides the official TIN registration portal:

https://tin.jtb.gov.ng/

How Individuals Get TIN Using NIN

If you already have a National Identification Number (NIN), you can generate your TIN automatically.

Steps:

  1. Visit the JTB TIN portal
  2. Select “Individual”
  3. Enter your NIN
  4. Your TIN will be generated automatically

How Businesses Get TIN

Companies and business entities can obtain a TIN using their CAC registration number.

Steps:

  1. Visit the TIN portal
  2. Select “Company”
  3. Enter CAC registration number
  4. The system retrieves your company tax profile

Why TIN Is Important

You may need a TIN for:

  • Opening business bank accounts
  • Government contracts
  • Tax filing
  • Business registration compliance
  • Investment transactions
