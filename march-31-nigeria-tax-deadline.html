Why March 31 Is the Most Important Tax Deadline in Nigeria

March 31 is one of the most important financial deadlines in Nigeria, yet many individuals and businesses overlook it.

This date represents the deadline for filing annual personal income tax returns for the previous year.

Who Needs to File Before March 31?

  • Salary earners
  • Freelancers and consultants
  • Business owners
  • Individuals with multiple income sources

Why This Deadline Is Important

With recent changes in Nigeria's tax system, compliance is becoming more data-driven and enforcement-focused.

Tax authorities now rely on multiple data sources to verify income declarations.

What Happens If You Miss the Deadline?

  • Penalties may apply
  • Additional tax assessments may be raised
  • Compliance notices may be issued

March vs April: What You Should Know

March is the compliance window. April is when enforcement activities typically begin.

How to Prepare Before March 31

  • Review all income sources
  • Obtain a Tax Identification Number (TIN)
  • Keep proper financial records
  • Understand applicable reliefs
