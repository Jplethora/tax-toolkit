Welcome to the Jerisma Plethora Nigeria Tax Hub. Everything below is updated for the Nigeria Tax Act 2025 (in force January 1, 2026).

  1. Get your TIN – free, 5 minutes on JTB portal
  2. Know your deadline – March 31 for individuals, Jan 31 for employers

  • CGT = now up to 25% for individuals, 30% for companies
  • Rent relief = 20% up to ₦500k
