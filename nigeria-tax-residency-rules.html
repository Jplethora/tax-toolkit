Residency rules

Why tax residency is one of the most important cross-border concepts.

Residency shapes how countries look at income, presence, and reporting. For Nigerians abroad, this is often the page that makes the rest of the discussion easier to understand.

Jerisma Plethora Team

Built for Nigerian residents, freelancers, business owners, and diaspora families who want the topic explained plainly before they pay for help.

Tax residency is not just about where someone feels at home. It is a legal and factual question that can influence which income becomes relevant in a jurisdiction and how filings are approached.

Factors that often matter

  • Time spent in Nigeria during the year.
  • Where employment duties or professional services are actually performed.
  • Where business and economic ties are strongest.
  • Where a permanent or regular place of residence exists.

Why diaspora readers care

People living abroad may still have Nigerian ties through family, property, business, or local income streams. Residency questions help frame how those ties should be understood.

Important: Residency analysis is often fact-specific. This page helps readers ask better questions, but it is not a substitute for case-based advice.
