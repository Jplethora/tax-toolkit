Nigeria Tax Information Toolkit
for Diaspora & Foreign Investors

This free toolkit is designed for Nigerians living abroad, members of the diaspora, and foreign investors who have financial interests, income, or assets connected to Nigeria.

It provides general tax information and educational guidance to help you better understand common tax-related considerations that may arise in cross-border situations.

What This Toolkit Covers

  • General awareness of Nigerian tax exposure related to foreign income
  • High-level considerations for investments, remittances, and Nigerian-based assets
  • Compliance awareness for Nigerians abroad and international stakeholders
  • Common tax questions faced by diaspora individuals and investors
