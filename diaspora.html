DIASPORA RESOURCES

Nigeria Tax Information Toolkit for Diaspora & Foreign Investors

This free toolkit is designed for Nigerians living abroad, members of the diaspora, and foreign investors who have financial interests, income, or assets connected to Nigeria. It provides general tax information and educational guidance to help you better understand common tax-related considerations that may arise in cross-border situations.

What This Toolkit Covers

Nigerian Tax Residency Rules

How Nigeria determines tax residency for citizens living abroad, and the critical difference between resident and non-resident status for tax purposes.

Foreign Income & Remittances

The treatment of income earned abroad, money sent home to family, and repatriated investment returns under current Nigerian tax law.

Double Taxation Agreements (DTAs)

An overview of Nigeria's tax treaties with the UK, Canada, South Africa, China, and others — and how they help prevent being taxed twice on the same income.

Property, Investments & Business Interests

Key compliance considerations for owning real estate, holding shares, or operating a business in Nigeria while living overseas.

Whether you're sending money home, managing rental income in Lagos or Abuja, or considering an investment in Nigerian markets, knowing the right questions to ask is the first step toward making informed financial decisions. This toolkit won't give you personalized advice — but it will equip you to have smarter conversations with your advisors on both sides of the border.
