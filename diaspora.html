Diaspora toolkit

A simpler tax orientation for Nigerians abroad and foreign investors.

This section gives cross-border readers a steady overview of the Nigerian tax questions that tend to appear around remittances, property, residency, and locally sourced income.

Jerisma Plethora Team

Built for Nigerian residents, freelancers, business owners, and diaspora families who want the topic explained plainly before they pay for help.

This toolkit is built for Nigerians who live outside the country but still have family commitments, assets, businesses, income, or investments linked to Nigeria. It is also useful for foreign investors who want a plain-language introduction before speaking to an adviser.

What this section covers

  • How locally sourced Nigerian income may create tax attention.
  • Why tax residency matters when income crosses borders.
  • How remittances and property ownership raise practical questions.
  • How to think about compliance before an issue becomes urgent.
Good starting point: If you earn abroad but still own property, receive Nigerian income, or support family members financially, the residency and remittance guides are worth reading next.
