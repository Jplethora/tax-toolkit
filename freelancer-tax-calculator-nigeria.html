Freelancer Tax Calculator (Nigeria)

If you earn from freelance work, remote jobs, or business, use this calculator to estimate your annual tax in Nigeria.

How to use

  • Enter your total annual income
  • Enter your business expenses (costs of running your work)
  • Click calculate to see your tax estimate

Example

Income: ₦3,000,000 → Expenses: ₦500,000 → Profit: ₦2,500,000 → Tax calculated after reliefs.

👉 Use PAYE calculator if you earn salary
