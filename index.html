Tax Update 2026: What Actually Changed

Three big shifts every Nigerian earner and business must know.

Individuals

₦800,000 now completely tax-free. New progressive bands: 15% up to ₦3M, 18% to ₦12M, 21% to ₦25M, 23% to ₦50M, 25% above. Rent relief up to ₦500k available.

SMEs

Small companies (turnover ≤₦50M) now pay 0% Companies Income Tax. VAT stays at 7.5% but compliance is stricter. No more multiple brackets.

Compliance

New unified filing deadlines, higher penalties for late PAYE, and mandatory TIN-linking for bank accounts. FIRS now auto-reconciles.
